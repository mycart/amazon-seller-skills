# Amazon Ads Benchmarks

Use these reference points when the source export does not provide a better
internal benchmark:

- Branded campaign ACOS: target under 5%
- ACOS versus target: pass within plus or minus 10%, warning at 10-25% over,
  fail above 25% over
- TACOS 90-day trend: improving if decreasing, caution if flat, problem if
  increasing
- Search-term harvest cadence: weekly is best, monthly is weak
- Sponsored Brands video coverage: active on top 10 ASINs is strong
- Brand Analytics access: weekly review is preferred

Always prefer the seller's real margin targets, account history, and category
economics over these fallback benchmarks.

# Amazon Ads Scoring System

Score the audit on a 0-100 weighted basis using the categories already defined
inside the skill:

- Campaign Structure and Portfolios: 15
- Search-Term Harvesting and Negatives: 25
- ACOS and TACOS Discipline: 20
- Bid and Budget Management: 15
- Sponsored Brands: 10
- Sponsored Display: 10
- Brand Analytics and Reporting: 5

For each category:

- 90-100: strong execution with only minor optimization gaps
- 70-89: workable but leaving meaningful efficiency or scale on the table
- 50-69: several structural issues that should be prioritized
- below 50: urgent operational problems or missing foundations

Combine the weighted category scores into the final Amazon Ads Health Score and
show the category breakdown in the report.
